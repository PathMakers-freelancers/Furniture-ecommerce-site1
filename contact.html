Support Desk

How can I book a design consultation?

Consultations can be booked directly through our platform after completing the initial project inquiry form. Our design concierge will reach out within 24 hours to schedule a session.

What regions do you currently serve?

While our primary studios are in NY, London, and Tokyo, we provide architectural and interior services globally, with active projects across Europe, Asia, and the Americas.

Do you offer professional installation?

Yes, every Modwood project includes professional white-glove installation managed by our lead architects to ensure the highest standards of execution.
